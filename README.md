## ✨ What is this?

**My Habits** is a beautiful, pastel-themed habit tracker that lives entirely in your browser — no app store, no account, no fuss. Open it in Safari on your iPhone, add it to your home screen, and it works just like a real app.

Built as a pure HTML/CSS/JS single file. No frameworks, no backend, no tracking.

---

## 📱 How to use on iPhone

1. Open the link in **Safari**
2. Tap the **Share** button → **Add to Home Screen**
3. Name it and tap **Add**
4. It opens full screen like a native app 🌸

## 🌟 Features

### Today Tab
- ✅ Tap to **check off habits** for the day
- 🌸 **Petal burst animation** when you tick something done
- 📖 **Notes** — hold (long press) any habit card to add a note for that day
- 📝 **Notes history** — tap the 📖 button to see all past notes for a habit
- ▶️ **Focus timer** per habit — start a session, stop when done, get an inspiring summary
- ⠿ **Drag to reorder** habits with the handle on the left
- ⏸ **Pause habits** temporarily without deleting them (great for travel)
- 😄 **Mood tracker** — log how you feel each day with 5 emoji levels

### Weekly Tab
- 📅 7-day completion grid (full / partial / missed)
- 📊 Completion rate for the week
- 📈 Per-habit progress bars with your all-time best streak
- 😊 Mood grid for the week with average
- ✨ **Weekly recap** — tap to see a full summary card with all your stats

### Monthly Tab
- 🗓️ Full calendar heatmap — green (all done), orange (partial), grey (missed)
- 🌿 Emoji icons of completed habits shown on each day
- 👆 Tap any day to see a detailed list of what you did and missed
- ✏️ Toggle habits on/off for past days directly from the detail view
- 📊 Monthly stats — total done, completion rate, perfect days

## 🎨 Customisation

- **9 pastel background themes** — Sakura, Lavender, Mint, Peach, Sky, Rose, Honey, Lilac, Cotton
- **🌙 Dark mode** — soft dark pastel theme, remembers your preference
- **✏️ Rename the app** — tap the pencil icon next to the title to give it your own name
- **10 habit colors** — full pastel palette including custom hex colors
- **23+ habit icons** — preset emoji grid plus a **+** tile to open your phone's full native emoji keyboard for any emoji you want

---

## 💾 Data & Backup

- All data saved locally in your browser — no servers, fully private
- **Export to CSV** — download all habit history as a spreadsheet
- **Manual backup** — download a full JSON backup anytime
- **Restore from backup** — load any JSON backup file
- **Weekly auto-backup** — app silently saves a backup every 7 days automatically, keeps the last 4 weeks
- **Restore auto-backup** — one-tap restore from the latest auto-save

## 🏆 Motivation Features

- 🔥 **Streak tracking** — current streak and all-time best per habit
